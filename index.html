FAQ

katwang7749@gmail.com!

What should I bring?

  • Any form of identification (School ID, health card, etc.) laptop, charger, drawing tablet, and anything you need to make a game
  • sleeping bag & whatever you need to spend the night
  • toiletries: toothbrush, toothpaste, deodorant, etc.
  • your favourite emotional support plush! (optional)

Am I eligible?

Are you 18 or under? If yes, you're eligible! If you have any doubts about your eligibility, send a quick email to katwang7749@gmail.com!

My parents are worried!

We're here to help calm any doubts! If they have specific questions, they can send an email to katwang7749@gmail.com, and make sure to have them check out:

Parent Guide

What can I make at ARCADE?

Any game you want, as long as it follows the theme: a probability-based arcade game. Have fun and get creative!

Do I have to pay?

No! All costs are covered by our lovely sponsors, which means you get free food, drinks, swag, and vibes! Just show up!

I'm new to coding, can I join?

Absolutely! There will be workshops to attend and you can ask peers too.
